The quip water flosser is a complex electromechanical system integrating a DC Motor, Lithium Ion Battery, Pump System with detailed stamped metal finishes. I was responsible for the design and refinement of the product from Alpha Prototype to Mass Production.

Key Activities:

  • Waterproofing and gasket refinement to achieve IPX7
  • Charging contact corrosion resistance and plating specifications
  • Metal stamping design and assembly
  • Step / gap minimization at key part junctions
  • Assembly process flow refinement for proper part alignment and centering

IPX7 waterproofing ensures full protection against water immersion. Extensive gasket refinement was carried out to maintain a complete seal while allowing for high-pressure water flow.

Color and material selection were key factors in the final industrial design, ensuring a premium feel while maintaining cost efficiency in manufacturing.

Sealing performance verification tests were conducted to evaluate the effectiveness of internal waterproof barriers.
